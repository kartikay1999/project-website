About Us

'Phantom Hands' is a metaphor for generations of artisans whose contributions manifest in each piece of furniture we make. After much thought, and investigations into its authorship claims and copyright situation - a journey that took them from Chandigarh to Geneva, via Montreal and Paris - Aparna and Deepak took the plunge and set up a small workshop in Bangalore to make handcrafted furniture. The first collection of furniture from Phantom Hands, Project Chandigarh, was launched in May 2015. Having started with three chair models, the collection has expanded to re-editions of several pieces of furniture that was originally designed for the city of Chandigarh under the guidance of Swiss architect Pierre Jeanneret.
